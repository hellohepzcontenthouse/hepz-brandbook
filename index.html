04 — Brand Voice

THE TONE

What HEPZ sounds like.

Confident but not cold. Warm but not soft. Direct without being blunt. Every word earns its place. Every number is real. If it could go on a supplement label, it does not belong in our writing.

01 / Direct
Say what you mean. Then stop.

No padding. No corporate words. Every sentence does one job. The words matter more than the word count. If you can cut it and nothing is lost, cut it.

In practice
"You focus on the business. We make sure the world sees it."
02 / Warm
Confidence with a human pulse.

HEPZ was built by a person, not a boardroom. That stays in everything we write. Authority does not have to cost warmth. We have both and we do not trade one for the other.

In practice
"Not sure? Just book a call. We'll tell you honestly which one fits, even if it's the smallest one."
03 / Strategic
Every claim is earned. Every number real.

We do not say "amazing results" when we can say "862K interactions." We do not say "we're different" without showing how. Strategy lives in the specifics, not the adjectives.

In practice
"From zero to 200K+ followers. Because we stayed consistent long enough for the platform to push the content without being asked."
04 / Faith-rooted
Built for delight. Grounded in purpose.

HEPZ is named after a word that means "my delight is in her." That meaning lives quietly in everything we do. Excellence, joy, light. Not preached. Just practised, every time.

In practice
"You are the light of the world. A city on a hill cannot be hidden."

What HEPZ never says

"We are passionate about..." "Best-in-class solutions" "Synergy" "Going viral" "Guaranteed results"

06 — Usage Rules

DO & DON'T

The logo is a system. Treat it like one.

Six rules. If something comes up that is not covered here, ask yourself one thing. Does this serve the brand or just the moment? If it is only the moment, do not do it.

✓ Do
Use on approved backgrounds only
V1 navy fill sits on white or light backgrounds. V3 reversed goes on dark or navy. V2 light is for branded documents. Do not put any version on a photograph.
✗ Don't
Never stretch, skew, or distort
Do not scale it non-proportionally. Do not rotate it. Do not add drop shadows, glows, or 3D effects to any part of the mark. It was drawn carefully. Keep it that way.
✓ Do
Keep the clearspace clear
The minimum breathing room around the logo on all four sides is the height of the H in HEPZ. Nothing comes inside that space. Not text, not other logos, not decorative elements.
✗ Don't
Never recolour the mark
Three versions. That is what exists. No gradients on the logo. No colours from outside the palette. No outlines added around the mark. If none of the three versions work, that is a layout problem, not a logo problem.
✓ Do
Use the favicon at small sizes
Below 48px, switch to the icon-only favicon mark. The text does not hold at that size. Do not force it. The house and the blue play button are enough.
✗ Don't
Never use on busy backgrounds
No photos. No patterns. No complex gradients behind any version. Always use a solid colour from the approved palette. If you are not sure, use white.
